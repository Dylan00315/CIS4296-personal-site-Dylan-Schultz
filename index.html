Restaurant Management System

I created a system that would allow for there to be a kitchen, server, and admin with all their own functions when it came to the restaurant. For the kitchen they had the option to say what food orders are complete and what food is not cooked, being cooked, or if a certain food item in the order is finished. For the server they could create an order and select whatever items that the customer ordered. For the admin they had the ability to do what the server and kitchen do along with the ability to do other things such as adding a new food item to the menu, being able to prioritize an order and that order to go to the to of the kitchen's to do list, and also the ability to take something off of the customers receipt.
